Food Nutrition For Fitness

The right amount and type of protein will help you shed some extra weight and will help you exercise better. Make sure you consume fish, seafood, skinless, white-meat poultry, skim or low-fat milk, skim or low-fat yogurt, low-fat cheese, eggs, and beans.
Vegetables

For those aiming to enhance their athletic performance, vegetables in your diet are essential. They provide an abundance of nutrients that fuel your body for physical activity.

Protein

Not only does eating protein help prevent muscle breakdown, but it can also help build muscles. Combining regular activity and exercise with protein intake promotes muscle growth.

Beans
